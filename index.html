About Community Volunteers

Community Volunteers is a student-led initiative aimed at encouraging individuals to give back to society through simple yet impactful volunteer activities.

Our goal is to foster responsibility, compassion, and teamwork while making a positive difference within the local community.

Our Volunteer Activities

Clean Up Playground

Date: 6/12/2025

Time: 8.00 am

Location: Taman Permainan Taman Kenari Jaya, Durian Tunggal

Join us in restoring a local playground by cleaning littered areas and creating a safer, cleaner environment for children and residents.

Feeding Stray Animals

Date: 20/12/2025

Time: 10.00 am

Location: Pekan Durian Tunggal

Help provide food to stray animals around the community. This activity promotes kindness, compassion, and awareness of animal welfare.

Online Logic Gates Tuition

Date: 3/1/2026

Time: 8.00 pm

Location: Online (Microsoft Teams)

An online tuition session focused on basic logic gates such as AND, OR, NOT, NAND, and NOR. This session helps students strengthen their understanding of digital logic concepts through guided explanations and examples.
