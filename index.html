About Community Volunteers

Community Volunteers is a student-led initiative aimed at encouraging individuals to give back to society through simple yet impactful volunteer activities.

Our goal is to foster responsibility, compassion, and teamwork while making a positive difference in the local community.

Our Volunteer Activities

Clean Up Playground

Join us in restoring a local playground by cleaning littered areas and creating a safer environment for children.

Feeding Stray Animals

Help provide food and basic care to stray animals, promoting compassion and animal welfare.

Community Sharing Session

Engage with community members through simple activities, games, and conversations.
